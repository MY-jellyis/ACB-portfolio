About Me

Hello! I’m Angelo C. Barbacena, a 4th year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at Mindoro State University. I’m a passionate designer and Literary Editor for Builders Pandayan, where I bring creativity to life through digital art and storytelling.


My focus lies in graphic design, illustration, and web development. I enjoy transforming ideas into interactive visuals — balancing creativity with functionality to craft meaningful user experiences.


Beyond design, I value teamwork, leadership, and continuous learning. I believe that growth comes from collaboration, passion, and curiosity for new technologies.
